# key_stats

A small Python tool to obtain a histogram of the characters you type into it

```shell
josephlyons@Josephs-MacBook-Pro key_stats % ./key_stats.py

Text: This is a fairly useless tool, but it is sort of fun.

        SPACE | 11 | ****************************************************************************************************
            s |  7 | ***************************************************************
            i |  5 | *********************************************
          t o |  4 | ************************************
        f l u |  3 | ***************************
        a r e |  2 | ******************
T h y , b n . |  1 | *********

Total Keys Entered: 53
```
